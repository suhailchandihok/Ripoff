About & FAQ
Where do prices come from?
All prices are real — sourced from the US Bureau of Labor Statistics Consumer Price Index (CPI). Updated monthly. BLS.gov is public domain government data.
How does the streak work?
Finish in the top 25% of players for the day and your streak survives. Miss it and your streak resets to 0. Results are calculated every night at midnight.
How is accuracy calculated?
Accuracy = how close your guess was to the real price as a percentage. 100% means you guessed exactly right. 0% means you were very far off.
Who wins the day?
One player per day — whoever had the closest guess (highest accuracy). In the event of a tie, the earlier submission wins.
